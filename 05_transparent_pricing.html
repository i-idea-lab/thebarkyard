Frequently Asked Joy

help_center What are your vaccination requirements?

Safety is our top priority. All guests must have current vaccinations for Rabies, Distemper/Parvo, and Bordetella. We also highly recommend Canine Influenza protection. You can easily upload records through our member portal.

help_center What is your cancellation policy?

We understand that plans change. You can cancel any daycare or grooming appointment up to 24 hours in advance without any fee. For our training courses, we offer a full refund if canceled 7 days prior to the first session.

help_center Do you offer multi-dog discounts?

Absolutely! We love seeing siblings play together. We offer a 15% discount for the second dog and a 20% discount for any additional dogs from the same household for all daycare packages.

help_center Can I tour the facilities first?

We encourage it! We offer free "Sniff and Seek" tours every Saturday morning. You can book your tour through the main navigation button to see our play areas, suites, and grooming stations.
